Shinkansen

The Shinkansen, often referred to as the bullet train, is Japan's world-famous high-speed rail system. First introduced in 1964, it revolutionized train travel with its speed, safety, and punctuality. The latest models can reach speeds of up to 320 km/h (200 mph).

  • Top speed: 320 km/h (200 mph)
  • Route: Tokyo, Osaka, Kyoto, and more
  • Travel time: Tokyo to Osaka in 2 hours 30 minutes
  • Features: Spacious seating, quiet carriages, on-board dining

The Shinkansen continues to be an icon of Japanese engineering, known for its impeccable safety record and reliability. Whether you're commuting between major cities or taking in the scenic countryside, the Shinkansen delivers a seamless and comfortable travel experience.
